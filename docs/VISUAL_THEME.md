The website should feel like a premium education brand for students aged 17–30—bright, approachable, and contemporary—while preserving the trust and credibility expected by parents making high-value education decisions.
Every page should resemble a modern education product rather than a traditional corporate website. Soft colored surfaces are the default canvas. White is a supporting color used only where it improves readability or interaction. Every viewport should contain visual warmth through pastel surfaces, subtle gradients, and varied icon treatments.

The website is built on colored surfaces.

Not white surfaces.

White is reserved for typography contrast, form controls,
and small content containers.

The page itself should always feel alive through soft color.
